  • A Quick History Lesson

    Our journey began in 1991, in the back of a small home office in Humble TX, a small town twenty-minutes north of downtown Houston. With a desire to offer high quality work at affordable prices, we slowly perfected the craft of paintless dent repair, practicing on throw-away junk hoods from wrecked cars in our garage, and then offering our neighbors free dent repair to get feedback on the quality of our work. After six months of practice we started soliciting our services to local rental car agencies and dealerships. By 1994 we were repairing our first hail damaged vehicles for a Honda dealership and quickly evolved to specializing in auto hail repair. In 1996 we set our focus full-time on "hail-chasing" - repairing thousands of vehicles for new car dealerships, body shops, and factories. Fast forward 28 years and our headquarters are still in Humble, TX, now running operations out of a 7,000 sq. ft. shop, still performing the best auto hail repairs today and following wherever the hail road takes us.
